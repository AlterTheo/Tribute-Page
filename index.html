Unsung Heroes of Bulacan

"They showed bravery and heroism in facing danger despite the perils to their own lives"

Names of the Heroes:

  • George Agustin from Iba O’ Este, Calumpit
  • Troy Justin Agustin from Sta. Rita, Guiguinto
  • Marby Bartlome from Bulihan, Malolos City
  • Jerson Resurreccion from Catmon, Santa Maria
  • Narciso Calayag Jr. were on board a truck on their way to rescue stranded families in Barangay Camias.
